## Month 1 Optimization Schedule

**Week 1 (Days 1–7):** Let campaigns run. Do not make changes. Collect impression and click data.

**Week 2 (Days 8–14):** Review Search Terms report. Add any irrelevant queries to negative keyword list. Pause any ad groups with 0 clicks and $20+ spend.

**Week 3 (Days 15–21):** Check Quality Scores. Any keyword below 5/10 needs ad copy or landing page improvement. Review device performance — if mobile CPL is 2x desktop, reduce mobile bid adjustment by 20%.

**Week 4 (Days 22–30):** Review campaign-level performance. Shift budget from lowest-performing campaign to highest-performing campaign. Prepare Month 2 A/B test plan.
