## Meta Ads — Pre-Launch Requirements

Before any Meta ads can run, complete these steps in order:

1. **Create Meta Business Manager** at business.facebook.com — use kyle@apollohomebuilders.com as the admin email
2. **Connect Facebook Page** — "Homes by Apollo" or "Apollo Home Builders" Page must be claimed and connected
3. **Connect Instagram Account** — link the @apollohomebuilders Instagram to Business Manager
4. **Add Payment Method** — credit card under the Apollo business name
5. **Create Meta Pixel** — Business Manager → Events Manager → Connect Data Sources → Web → Create Pixel → Name it "Apollo Website Pixel"
6. **Get Pixel ID** — copy the 15-16 digit number from Events Manager → your Pixel → Settings
7. **Activate Pixel on website** — send Pixel ID to Kyle/WorkPlay; the code is already in apollohomebuilders.com's index.html with a placeholder — just replace `YOUR_PIXEL_ID_HERE` with the actual ID and redeploy
8. **Complete 10DLC / Business Verification** — Meta will ask you to verify the business before spending above $50/day. Have your EIN and business address ready.

**Estimated setup time:** 45–60 minutes

## Campaign 1 — Awareness (Facebook + Instagram) — $400/month

**Goal:** Reach Las Vegas metro buyers who are not yet actively searching. Build brand recognition before they go to Google.

**Audience:**
- Location: Las Vegas DMA + 60-mile radius from Pahrump, NV
- Age: 30–65
- Household income: Top 40% (use "Financial" behavioral targeting)
- Interests: Real estate, home buying, home improvement, Nevada living, retirement planning, desert lifestyle
- Exclude: People who already visited apollohomebuilders.com (retargeting handles them separately)

**Ad Format:** Video (15–30 seconds) + Static Image (for A/B test)

**Ad Copy — Version A (Lifestyle):**
> **Headline:** More Home. Less Money. Pahrump, NV.
> **Primary Text:** Las Vegas buyers are discovering something: 60 miles west, you can get a brand-new 3-bedroom home with land, privacy, and room to breathe — for half the price. Homes by Apollo builds new construction homes in Pahrump starting from $350K. Schedule a free consultation today.
> **CTA Button:** Learn More → apollohomebuilders.com

**Ad Copy — Version B (Value):**
> **Headline:** New Construction Homes in Pahrump, NV — From $350K
> **Primary Text:** Stop competing for overpriced resale homes in Las Vegas. Homes by Apollo builds quality new construction in Pahrump, NV — 3 and 4 bedroom floor plans, custom options, and a local team that knows every lot in the valley. See what's available.
> **CTA Button:** See Homes → apollohomebuilders.com/find-your-home

**Creative Direction:**
The single most important creative asset for this campaign is a 20–30 second video showing: (1) an aerial shot of Pahrump with mountains in the background, (2) a walkthrough of a finished home interior, (3) a family on the lot or porch, (4) end card with the Homes by Apollo logo and "From $350K." If no video exists yet, use a carousel of 4–5 high-quality property photos. Real photos outperform stock images by 3–5x in real estate ads.

## Google Business Profile — Setup & Optimization Checklist

Google Business Profile (GBP) is free and drives significant local search traffic. A fully optimized GBP can generate 5–15 leads per month at zero ad spend.

### Step 1 — Create the Profile (15 min)
Go to [business.google.com/create](https://business.google.com/create) and complete:
- **Business Name:** Homes by Apollo (or Apollo Home Builders — be consistent with what you want to rank for)
- **Category:** Home Builder (primary) + New Home Builder (secondary)
- **Address:** Use your Pahrump office or model home address. If you do not have a fixed address, select "Service Area Business" and enter Pahrump, NV 89048
- **Phone:** (775) 910-7771
- **Website:** https://apollohomebuilders.com
- **Service Area:** Pahrump, NV + Nye County, NV

### Step 2 — Verify the Profile (1–3 business days)
Google will send a postcard with a verification code to your address, or may offer phone/email verification. Complete this before doing anything else — an unverified profile will not rank.

### Step 3 — Complete the Profile (30 min)
Once verified, fill in every field:

**Business Description (750 characters max — use all of it):**
> Homes by Apollo is Pahrump's local new home builder, specializing in quality new construction homes and residential lots in Nye County, Nevada. We offer 3 and 4 bedroom floor plans starting from $350K, custom build options, and lot-plus-build packages. Unlike national builders, we know every lot in the Pahrump Valley — the soil, the utilities, the permit process, and the best locations for your lifestyle. Our team provides personal service from first consultation through move-in day. Schedule a free consultation today at apollohomebuilders.com.

**Services to Add:**
- New Home Construction
- Custom Home Building
- Residential Lot Sales
- Lot and Build Packages
- Floor Plan Consultation
- Free Home Buyer Consultation

**Hours:**
- Monday–Friday: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM (if applicable)
- Sunday: Closed

**Attributes to Enable:**
- Identifies as veteran-owned (if applicable)
- Online appointments
- Free consultation

### Step 4 — Upload Photos (Priority Order)

Google profiles with 10+ photos receive 35% more clicks than those with fewer. Upload in this order:

| Photo Type | Quantity | What to Show |
|---|---|---|
| Exterior — finished homes | 5–8 | Front elevation, driveway, landscaping |
| Interior — kitchen/living | 4–6 | Open floor plans, finishes, natural light |
| Aerial / lot views | 2–3 | Land, mountain views, neighborhood context |
| Team / personal | 2–3 | Kyle and Brandon on site or in the model home |
| Logo | 1 | The Homes by Apollo owl logo (square format) |
| Cover photo | 1 | Best exterior shot, landscape orientation |

### Step 5 — Add Posts (Ongoing, 2x per week)

GBP Posts appear in search results and keep the profile active. Use these post types:

- **New listing:** "New home available in Pahrump — 3 bed, 2 bath, from $[price]. View details at apollohomebuilders.com/find-your-home"
- **Offer:** "Free 2026 Pahrump Home Buyer's Guide — download at apollohomebuilders.com/pahrump-home-buyers-guide"
- **Update:** "Construction update: The [Home Name] at [Street] is framing complete. Move-in estimated [Month]."
- **Event:** "Free home buyer workshop — [Date] in Pahrump. Reserve your spot at apollohomebuilders.com/get-in-touch"

### Step 6 — Collect Reviews (Ongoing)

Google Reviews are the single highest-impact action for local search ranking. Target 10 reviews in the first 30 days.

**How to ask:** After any positive conversation, send this text message:
> "Hi [Name], it was great talking with you today. If you have 2 minutes, a Google review would mean a lot to us — it helps other Pahrump families find us. Here's the direct link: [your GBP review link]. Thank you! — Kyle, Homes by Apollo"

Get your direct review link from: GBP Dashboard → Get More Reviews → Copy Link.

**Who to ask first:** Past clients, contractors you have worked with, neighbors, anyone who has toured a home. Do not ask for reviews in exchange for discounts — this violates Google's terms.
