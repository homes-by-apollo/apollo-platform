**WHAT IS SCOPS**

SCOPS is our internal command center. Every lead, property, floor plan, and campaign lives here. It's what we use to run daily sales and customer operations — no spreadsheets, no sticky notes, no chasing each other for updates.

---

**THE 6 TABS**

**Dashboard** — Your morning briefing. KPIs, channel performance, and the activity feed. Start here every day.

**Pipeline** — Kanban board of every active lead. Deals at Risk is at the top — any lead showing red needs a follow-up call within the hour. Drag cards to move leads through stages. Click a card to see the full profile, schedule a tour, or add a note.

**Inventory** — Every home and lot we have. 42 listings live right now. The map clusters nearby pins so you can spot geographic concentration at a glance. Use it to show buyers what's available in their target area.

**Floor Plans** — The admin table for our plan catalog. You can update images, pricing, and PDF links here without touching the database.

**Campaigns** — Marketing hub. Blog posts, UTM links for our ads, email sequence management, and the Lead Magnets board showing traffic and conversion rates for each lead capture page. Ads go live this week — watch the Channel Performance chart as leads start coming in.

**Engine** — Our automations. New lead welcome emails, stale lead alerts, tour reminders. These run on their own. You don't need to touch this daily, but it's good to know what's firing.

**YOUR DAILY 5-MINUTE ROUTINE**

1. Open Dashboard — scan KPIs and the activity feed.
2. Open Pipeline — check Deals at Risk and call any flagged lead immediately.
3. Move any leads whose stage has changed to the correct column.

That's it. If a lead hasn't been contacted in 48 hours, you'll also get an email from the stale lead alert system.
